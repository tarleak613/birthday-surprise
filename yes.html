💌 A Letter (Kind of...)

hi som, We’ve known each other for years now. And honestly, I don’t think I’ve ever said it properly — but yeah, I care about you more than I let on. Inside Out, Notting Hill, 50 First Dates, Elementals… though let’s be honest, you slept through most of them 😂 Still, I liked having you there. Even if your eyes were closed 80% of the time. You know how you talk about your day — you light up. Genuinely excited to share every random little detail. It’s cute. You probably don’t even realize how easily you pull people in when you do that. I once had a picture of you that I really adored — one of those rare moments where everything just felt... right. Lost it when my phone broke. Just my luck. But the memory’s stubborn, it stayed. I’m way too good at writing long romantic letters (dangerous talent, I know), but I’ll keep this brief. I don’t want you falling for me or anything… so just this:

"I’m here. And I always will be."
yours rudely,
ayush
